```mermaid
flowchart TD
  A[Start: define Rich Life priorities] --> B[System: 4 pillars banking saving budgeting investing]
  B --> C[Autopilot: automatic transfers and bill pay]

  G -- No --> H[Skip]

  I --> J
  J --> C
```
